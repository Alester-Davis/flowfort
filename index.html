The Problem

The OT security gap
is getting worse.

Industrial environments were never designed with cybersecurity in mind. As OT systems connect to IT networks, the attack surface explodes — and legacy patching processes can't keep pace.

Manual, error-prone patching
80% of OT patches applied manually — missed updates, human error, and extended vulnerability windows attackers exploit.
Unplanned downtime and production loss
Uncoordinated patch deployment disrupts production schedules, triggers safety shutdowns, and costs manufacturers millions in lost output.
Compliance without visibility
Proving compliance with IEC 62443, NERC CIP, and NIS2 requires audit trails that manual processes simply cannot reliably produce.
